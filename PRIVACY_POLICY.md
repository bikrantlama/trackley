# Privacy Policy — Trackley

**Last updated: April 20, 2025**
**Effective date: April 20, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how Trackley ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the Trackley mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information** — your name and email address when you register
- **Profile data** — display name, bio, profile photo (optional)
- **Habit data** — habit names, completion records, reminder times
- **Fitness data** — weight, height, age, gender, fitness goals (optional)
- **Financial data** — transaction amounts, labels, and categories you manually enter
- **Content** — progress photos, group names, and notes you create

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Step count** — via your device's built-in pedometer (processed on-device, not transmitted)
- **Notification delivery data** — whether you have opened notification-triggered app sessions
- **App usage data** — features used, screens visited (anonymised)

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect your location
- We do **not** collect biometric data
- We do **not** sell your data to third parties
- We do **not** run behavioural advertising

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---|---|

| Syncing your data across devices | Contract performance |
| Sending habit reminder notifications | Consent (you enable these) |
| Detecting and fixing bugs | Legitimate interest |

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- **Cloud storage**: Your account data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can read or write your own data.
- **Local storage**: A local cache is maintained on your device via AsyncStorage for offline access.
- **Encryption**: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+. Data at rest is encrypted by Firebase/Google Cloud.
- **Retention**: Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share your data only in these limited circumstances:

- **Firebase / Google** — our backend provider processes data to operate the service. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Legal requirements** — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Business transfers** — in the event of a merger or acquisition (you will be notified).

We **never** sell your personal data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Trackley is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access** — request a copy of your data
- **Correction** — update any inaccurate data via the Profile screen
- **Deletion** — delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen
- **Portability** — request your data in a portable format
- **Objection** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdrawal of consent** — disable notifications at any time in device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

---

## 8. Notifications

We send push notifications only for:
- Habit reminders (times you configure)
- Achievement unlocks

You may disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app.
